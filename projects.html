Fleetrace

Fleetrace is a web-based internal live vehicle tracking system designed to help organizations efficiently monitor and manage their vehicles in real time. The system enables administrators to track the live geographic location of vehicles using GPS coordinates visualized on Leaflet.js. Fleetrace is intended strictly for internal administrative use, making it suitable for driving schools, training institutes, and small organizations that require a simple and cost-effective tracking solution.
